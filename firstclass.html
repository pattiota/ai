ERP Custom Office Furnishings Simulation

From: Patti Ota  |  Date: August 26, 2025  |  Class: MIS 427/527

Purpose & Overview

This simulation was designed to help students experience how integrated business processes function in a real-world organization, serving as an introduction to ERP concepts through hands-on learning.

Key Takeaways

ERP systems greatly improve efficiency when information is centralized and accessible across departments
Communication between teams is critical, especially in early stages before technology bridges the gaps
Real-time data visibility helps prevent delays and errors in production and purchasing
